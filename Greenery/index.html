What is Greenery?

We believe that even having a single plant in our homes, offices, or anywhere else will be a huge help to our environment and nature, especially now that the world is experiencing climate change. Plants not only benefit the environment, but they also add joy and ambiance to our homes and offices. Greenery is an e-commerce platform that sells house and office plants, seeds, and offers indoor landscaping, which is also known as plantscaping, through partnerships with small and medium-sized enterprises.

The Greenery sells a wide range of plants, including:

  • Foliage Plants
  • Succulent Plants
  • Fern Plants

The Greenery Also Offers Plantscaping Services!

We offers you a variety of options for incorporating plants into your indoor and outdoor environments, which will undoubtedly add a new ambiance to your space.

Our Best Sellers

Money Tree

Pachira aquatica (Money tree)is a sought-after tree that grows exceptionally well indoors. A properly placed Pachira aquatica in the home or office will generate positive energy/chi, bringing prosperity and good luck, according to fengshui.

Echeveria Perle Von Nurnberg

The delicate display of pure pastel perfection is formed by the pale baby-blue leaves that are gently tipped with a subtle pink blush. Because of its compact size and ease of care, the Echeveria Perle Von Nurnberg is a welcome addition to windowsills, shelves, and desks alike.

Jade Plant

The Jade Plant, also known as the Money Plant, is a popular succulent houseplant that has been found for generations on shelves and bookcases. (This is not to be confused with the Money Tree, another common name for Pachira aquatica.)

Peace Lily

Spathiphyllum wallisii Bellini is a small Peace Lily with gracefully arching stems and long-lasting blooms. Its small, compact size makes it ideal for a small table or book shelf.

Rubber Plant

Ficus elastica Abidjan stands out with its rich bronze and burgundy leaf color. Ficus elastica make excellent ornamental house plants, and the colors of Ficus elastica Abidjan may be just what you're looking for.

ZZ Plant

The ZZ Plant, also known as the Zanzibar Gem, is a very popular and trendy house plant. Its easy care requirements make it an excellent choice for forgetful houseplant owners, and its stunning and unique foliage adds to its eye-catching appeal.
