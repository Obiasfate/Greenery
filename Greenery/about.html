What is Greenery?

We believe that even having a single plant in our homes, offices, or anywhere else will be a huge help to our environment and nature, especially now that the world is experiencing climate change. Plants not only benefit the environment, but they also add joy and ambiance to our homes and offices. Greenery is an e-commerce platform that sells house and office plants, seeds, and offers indoor landscaping, which is also known as plantscaping, through partnerships with small and medium-sized enterprises.

The Greenery sells a wide range of plants, including:

  • Foliage Plants
  • Succulent Plants
  • Fern Plants

Our Background

The Greenery Co. was inspired to address the recurring issue of climate change and other environmental problems by developing an ecommerce website that will form partnerships with Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Metro Manila, Philippines that sell plants and other plant-related products.

Our Mission

The Greenery Co.'s mission is to promote environmental sustainability by delivering plants to your home, office, or personal space at the lowest possible cost, with the highest quality products and services, and the greatest convenience.

Our Vision

The Greenery Co.'s vision is to be the most customer-centric online ecommerce platform for different kinds of plants and seeds companies in the Philippines, where clients can find and discover high-quality plants and seeds, as well as plantscaping services to purchase online. On our online platform, we also aim to provide an accessible, reliable, and enjoyable online shopping experience.
